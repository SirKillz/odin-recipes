Lasagna

Tasty fresh Italian Classic

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound anchovy fillets
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine the sausage, anchovy, garlic salt, oregano, and pepper in a large skillet; cook over medium heat until evenly browned, about 5 minutes. Drain and set aside.
